{% blocktrans %}Why staying at Miroslav Apartmany & Studia{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Our accommodation facility with sauna, surrounded by a river, trees and a spacious garden is located in the opening of one of the most beautiful valleys in Slovakia - Demanovska valley in Low Tatras - at the end of which, there's a well-known ski-centre JASNA Chopok. We are also close to the biggest Aquapark in Slovakia - Tatralandia. In summer, hiking and mountain biking is highly sought after in this area.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}What to do in Demanovska Valley{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Apartmany & Studia Miroslav are located in Demanovska Valley in Low Tatras, nature's paradise with plenty of possibilities to enjoy your vacation in as relaxed or as active way as you prefer. Besides being home to the biggest ski center in Slovakia - Jasna Chopok - and to Slovkia's longest cave system, the valley is the perfect starting point for countless hikes, trips to aquaparks Tatralandia and Besenova, thermal spas, historical sites or museums.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'HIKING & CYCLING' %}

{% blocktrans %}Tatra mountains offer a large number of trails for hiking and cycling with different levels of difficulty, from the demanding, which requires a certain degree of preparation to those that are designed for families with children or beginners. Apartmany & Studia Miroslav are the perfect hub!{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'SKIING' %}

{% blocktrans %}Located just next to the ski resorts of Jasna Chopok Nizke Tatry and Ziarce, you'll be able to minimize travelling time and enjoy your skiing on Slovakia's longest ski slopes even more. We also provide a storage room for all your equipment including heating racks for your ski boots.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'THERMAL' %}

{% blocktrans %}All within 20km, you can find the aquaparks Tatralandia and Besenova, thermal swimming pools in Liptovsky Jan and the recreation centre of Liptovsky Mara dam which together offer a variety of adrenaline watersports activities and opportunities to relax.{% endblocktrans %}
